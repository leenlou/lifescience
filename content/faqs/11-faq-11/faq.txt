Question: How do I prepare for my appointment?

----

Answer: 

The more we know about you, the better! We always begin with a comprehensive assessment of your health history, current lifestyle and state of wellness. You need to fill up a form with all the personal information necessary to give your doctor an accurate health profile. From there, your conversation and your journey with us can begin.
