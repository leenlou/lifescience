Text: 

Good health starts in our gut; it is where digestion and nutrient absorption happens. Our gut supports the immune system. This test measures antibodies that may be linked to inflammatory conditions in the body that cause a range of health issues.

* Digestive Stool Analysis
* Microbiology
* Parasitology
* Intestinal Permeability
* Gut Immunology
* Yeast Culture
* IgG Antibodies
* Celiac and Gluten Sensitivity
