Title: Nutritionist - Dietitian

----

Experience: Associate / Supervisor

----

Description: 

**Minimum Qualifications**
- Female
- Pleasing Personality
- Weight must be commensurate to height
- Physically Fit
- Willing to work on shifting schedule
- Possesses excellent nutrition counseling skills
- Licensed Nutritionist and Diettitian (with valid PRC license)
- With 1 - 2 years work experience in a reputable institution, hospital or healthcare facility.
- Acquires excellent written and verbal communication skills

**Job Description**
The ideal candidate will assess nutritional needs, diet restrictions and current health plans to develop and implement dietary care plans and provide nutritional counseling in accordance to Center standards.
