We begin with an in-depth Functional Medicine Consultation where our medical practitioner will chat with you and listen to your personal health goals, discuss your medical history and current lifestyle.

**PRObe**
Your initial conversation with our doctors give them an idea of what diagnostic tests you should consider in order to gain a deeper understanding of your body and identify system imbalances that need to be restored. The results of your consultation and your diagnostic tests give us a comprehensive profile of your current health status.  Now we can design a customized program that can be applied to your daily routine and guides you to a more sustainable approach towards your health goal.

No one knows your path to optimal health better than LifeScience!
